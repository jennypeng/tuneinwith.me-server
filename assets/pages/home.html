Project name

tune in with.

Friends let friends choose songs. Tune in with me for a democratic party music experience.

tuneinwith.me is closed alpha right now. Check back soon!

What’s this?

tuneinwith.me is a real-time collaborative music streaming web app that allows all Spotify desktop users to create private listening parties. Friends can simply visit a tuneinwith.me URL on their phones to add a song to the playing queue.

Quickstart

  1. Install the tuneinwith.me Spotify app.
  2. Choose a room name and switch rooms.
  3. Share the resulting link with your friends.
  4. Search and queue music from the app or website and get playing!

About the Project

tuneinwith.me was created at hackTECH 2014 by a group of UC Berkeley students with a passion for listening to music and being choosy about it. We’re so excited about our hack that we’re going to make this a real thing!
